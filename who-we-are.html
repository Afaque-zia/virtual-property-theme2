Who We Are?

Virtual Property Solution is a trusted name in the real estate industry, proudly headquartered in Wakad, Pune. Established with a vision to redefine urban living, we specialize in designing and delivering premium residential and commercial spaces that seamlessly blend functionality, innovation, and aesthetic appeal.

With years of expertise and a deep understanding of the evolving real estate landscape, we are committed to providing unparalleled value and quality. Each project we undertake reflects our dedication to excellence, sustainability, and customer-centricity. Guided by our core values of integrity, transparency, and innovation, we aim to exceed expectations at every step of the journey.

Our team of seasoned professionals works tirelessly to ensure every client experience is seamless, rewarding, and fulfilling. Whether you are looking to buy your dream home, establish a new commercial space, or expand your investment portfolio, Virtual Property Solution is your trusted partner.

Beyond just building properties, we build relationships, trust, and a brighter future for our clients. With a focus on modern design, strategic locations, and world-class amenities, we are shaping the future of real estate in Pune and beyond.

1. Residential Properties

Rental & Outright Sales: Flats, row houses, penthouses, villas, and residential plots ranging from 500 sq. ft. to acres.
Premium Properties: Exclusive flats priced between ₹20 lakh and ₹60 crore in posh areas developed by reputed builders.

2. Commercial Properties

Rental & Outright Sales: Offices, IT setups, shops, and showrooms.
Preleased Properties: Commercial offices, showrooms, and hotels with investment options from ₹2 crore to ₹50 crore, offering an ROI of 6%-9%.

3. Hospitality Properties

Rental & Outright: Executive to 5-star hotels, serviced apartments, restaurants, resto-bars, and suitable spaces for veg/non-veg dining establishments.

4. Transferable Development Rights (TDR)

We specialize in handling TDR deals in Hinjewadi / Wakad / Tathawade / Punawale / Ravet / Kiwale (PCMC) Baner/ Balewadi / Bavdhan.
